Bio

Hi, I'm Mac, a sound designer from Santa Barbara CA. Thank you for taking the time to explore my work!

I have a strong passion for immersive sound design, and an absolute love for storytelling through creative audio. As someone with a fantasy-driven mind, and a proclivity for tinkering, I thrive on the technical and creative aspects of audio production.

In the last year I moved to Los Angeles, and have since worked as a freelance sound designer & post-production mix engineer. Through my work, I have also found a secondary passion for the collaborative nature of on-set recording/mixing.

Main Skills: Sound Design, Pro Tools, Logic, iZotope RX, Field Recording, Production Mixing, Post-Production Mixing, Music/Score Production, Mastering.

Skills

Sound Design

  • Immersive Soundscapes with Crisp, Vibrant Audio
  • Emotionally Resonant Sound to enhance the drama, tension, and excitment of every scene

Post-Production Mixing

  • Crystal-Clear Definition: Perfectly balanced mixes to meet the highest industry standards
  • Collaborative Workflow: Clear communication and regular reviews to ensure the final product aligns with your creative vision

Production Recording & Foley

  • Pristine on-set audio capture using state-of-the-art equipment and expertise
  • Custom foley artistry to bring authentic depth to every clonk, bang, and pop

Original Score

  • Tailored Compositions to Develop music that supports and elevates your story's emotional core
  • Versatile Styles to accompany any genre, motif, or vibe
