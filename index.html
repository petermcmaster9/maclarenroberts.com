Bio

Hi, I'm Mac, a sound designer from Santa Barbara CA. Thank you for taking the time to explore my work!

I have a strong passion for immersive sound design, and an absolute love for storytelling through creative audio. As someone with a fantasy-driven mind and a proclivity for tinkering, I thrive on both the technical and creative aspects of audio production.

A 2023 Graduate of Los Angeles Recording School, I have since worked as a freelance sound designer & post-production mix engineer for numerous films & trailers. Through my work, I have also found a passion for the collaborative nature of production mixing.

My areas of expertise: Sound Design, Pro Tools, Logic, iZotope RX, Field Recording, Production Mixing, Post-Production Mixing, Music/Score Production, Mastering.

Skills

Digital Audio Workstation (DAW) Mastery

  • Expert in Pro Tools, Logic Pro, Ableton Live, iZotope
  • Fast-paced editor, comfortable working under tight deadlines

Synthesis, Sampling, & Sound Design

  • Creation of new sounds using synthesizers or manipulating recorded samples.
  • Detailed and accurate sound design and/or foley of anything ranging from simple footsteps to sci-fi creature & technology audio.

Sound Editing & Mixing

  • Ability to edit, clean, and mix audio for clarity, balance, and desired effect.
  • Collaborative Workflow: Clear communication with regular reviews to ensure the final product aligns with your creative vision.

Audio Recording Techniques

  • Capable of complex foley recording & sound design techniques
  • Knowledge of microphone types, placement, and field/studio recording methods
